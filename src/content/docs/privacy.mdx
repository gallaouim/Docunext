## Advertising and Data Collection
Our app uses third-party advertising services to display ads. To provide personalized 
and relevant advertisements, we and our advertising partners may collect and process 
the following information:
### Information Collected for Advertising:
- **Advertising Identifier (IDFA)**: A unique identifier assigned to your device by 
  Apple, used to deliver interest-based advertising and measure ad performance.
- **IP Address**: Used to estimate your general geographic location (country/city) 
  for regional ad targeting.
- **Device Information**: Including device model, operating system version, and 
  mobile network information.
- **App Usage Data**: Information about how you interact with ads within the app.
### Third-Party Advertising Partner:
We use **Appodeal** as our advertising mediation platform. Appodeal works with 
multiple ad networks to serve ads in our app. 
For more information about how Appodeal handles your data, please review their 
privacy policy: [Appodeal Privacy Policy](https://appodeal.com/privacy-policy/)
### Your Choices:
- **iOS 14.5+**: You can control whether apps can track your activity across other 
  companies' apps and websites by managing the "Allow Apps to Request to Track" 
  setting in your device's Privacy settings.
- **Opt-out of personalized ads**: You can limit ad tracking in your device settings 
  (Settings > Privacy > Advertising > Limit Ad Tracking on iOS).
### Data Retention:
Advertising data is retained according to the policies of our advertising partners. 
Please refer to the Appodeal Privacy Policy for specific retention periods.
