## Privacy Policy

Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our mobile applications available on the **Apple App Store** and **Google Play Store** (the “App”).

By using the App, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

## Information We Collect

### 1. Personal Information
We do **not** require you to provide personal information unless it is necessary for a specific feature.  
If you choose to contact us or sign in, we may collect:
- Name
- Email address
- User ID (if authentication is used)

### 2. Usage Data
We may collect non-personal information automatically, including:
- App interactions
- Device type and operating system
- App version
- Crash logs and performance data

This data helps us improve app stability and user experience.

### 3. Device Information
The App may access:
- Device identifiers (such as advertising ID)
- Language and region settings

No data is used to personally identify you.
